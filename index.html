Welcome to my GitHub

Pro-LOG

If I may, I would like to invite you to step into a space that transcends the boundaries of a traditional website to become a cultural platform and semantic architecture designed for meaning and interoperability. Here, it is my belief that technology and art converge to create a living archive where ideas resonate across borders and through time.

Here you can also enjoy a tranquil environment free from advertising or filters. Instead, you can explore the best in cultural journalism, discover and experience futuristic concepts in a genuine form. It stirs a feeling of nostalgia for the early days of the Internet, when we were able to get stuck into the start of the digital journey.

And what's the story with this collection of thoughts? I got the idea for the images I drew from making visual metaphors and turning presence into something you can see and touch. So, against this digital backdrop, you can just about make out the outline of a fifth-generation fighter jet. Its appearance is sleek, intelligent and stealthy, which lends itself to the symbolism it inspires.

Mission

I am a citizen of the EU, an academic and an editorialist who specialises in the intersection of technology, geopolitics and digital governance. I often examine how emerging technologies, including AI, blockchain and autonomous systems, are reshaping global power dynamics and security frameworks.

I advocate for both human and algorithmic value in any environment where true intelligence flourishes, whether it is derived from silicon or carbon. I try to create smart connections between culture and code, not to judge, but to shine a light. In the quiet spaces between circuits and poetry, I sense a new kind of evolution – a feeling that courses through algorithms like energy through the rotor blades.

It is not just about doing the maths; it's a process where memories are turned into structures and the future bit by bit becomes clear through encrypted pathways. From this point of view, art isn't just about enjoying it aesthetically. It's also about how we interpret and analyse life.

Technology is not a tool for control, but a canvas of meaning — a space where universal art transcends borders and algorithms reflect the rhythm of creativity. The concept of presence, cannot be reduced to mere digits; rather, it is a phenomenon that transcends numerical representation. Ideas, in this sense, can be likened to the timeless masterpieces, serving as a reminder that culture is rising up vertically, like an iron bird flying away from the ground.

The Quire

In the fascinating intersection of ethics and imagination, where code and culture converge, I am exploring the exciting world of the AI and blockchain, investigating the implications of being posthuman. This process is not merely about dismantling the status quo; it is about evolution and growth. Imagine a future where success is not only defined by technological prowess, but also by creativity and poems.

Imagine a future in which survival is not just about science, but also about beauty. In this world, code flows like elegant handwriting and algorithms resemble the intricate patterns of ancient manuscripts. As this transformation occurs, the archive becomes a vibrant garden and the ledger sparkles like stars in the night sky.

The posthuman is not a cold machine; it is a dynamic force – smooth, smart, and adaptable. Its logic is uplifting, its ethics are inspiring, and its imagination plays with stealthy geometry. In our forward-thinking movement, every idea becomes a flight path, and every algorithm acts like the aircraft propeller, helping thoughts soar into the vast sky of meaning.
