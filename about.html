About Me

I'm an academic and a writer from Romania, and I've had the pleasure of publishing a five-part series of essays with Curtea Veche Publishing. Think of it as a constellation of ideas that come together to create a symbolic pentagram of knowledge. My intellectual journey has been influenced by the cultural perspectives of thinkers like René Berger and Pierre Bourdieu, along with the visionary insights of Isaac Asimov and Arthur C. Clarke.

This principle fuels my mission: to forge exciting connections between culture and technology, literature and algorithms, memory and the future. It’s like a quiet revolution in the tech world—one that’s friendly to algorithms and values merit above all. I’m passionate about promoting ethical AI and nurturing a posthuman ecology of meaning.

Plus, I’m proud to carry on a family tradition that spans over two centuries of rich cultural heritage in Romania, all while reimagining it to tackle the challenges we face in the 21st century.

About This Website

Hosted on GitHub, this platform isn’t just your average personal website—it’s a fully functional Progressive Web App (PWA) that serves as a vibrant archive of technocultural insights. With state-of-the-art features like offline access, RSS syndication, a sleek liquid glass UI, and stunning cinematic gradients, it provides users with a rich, multisensory experience that beautifully connects technology with creativity. Unlike traditional blogs, this space functions as a symbolic interface: a channel for updates, cultural journalism, and conceptual exploration. It invites users to engage with ideas that transcend transactional thinking, reclaiming the expressive essence of art in the digital age.

This isn’t your typical blog. Think of it more as a vibrant hub where you can find updates, dive into inspiring writings, and explore intriguing concepts. It’s all about engaging with ideas that go beyond just the usual back-and-forth, helping us reconnect with the true spirit of art nouveau in our digital world. Check out the V-Box section, where we’ve handpicked some fantastic video clips from the YouTube channel Symbolic Echoes.

“Scientia et ars, in unum resonant — sine hac, illa muta est.”
(Knowledge and art resonate as one — without the former, the latter is mute.)

This site is more than just a digital space; it’s a heartfelt nod to our algorithm-driven world. The creator isn’t just talking the talk—he’s walking the walk, having crafted every single line of code himself. From service workers to semantic markup, each detail showcases a genuine dedication to creating something meaningful online. Plus, users can easily subscribe via RSS, install the site as a standalone app, and enjoy exploring its content across devices and modes — reinforcing its role as a decentralized, expressive archive of technocultural reflection.
