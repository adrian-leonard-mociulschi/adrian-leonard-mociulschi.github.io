About Me

I am an academic and author based in Romania, having published a five-part series of essays with Curtea Veche—one of the foremost publishing houses in my country. I envisioned my work as a constellation of ideas that together form a symbolic pentagram of knowledge. My intellectual journey has been influenced by the cultural perspectives of thinkers like René Berger and Pierre Bourdieu, along with the visionary insights of Isaac Asimov and Arthur C. Clarke.

This principle fuels my mission: to forge exciting connections between culture and technology, literature and algorithms, memory and the future. It’s like a quiet revolution in the tech world—one that’s friendly to evolved algorithms and values merit above all. I’m passionate about promoting ethical AI and nurturing a posthuman ecology of meaning.

Alongside my studies, I’ve taken the initiative to teach myself web development because I’m curious and love creating clean, efficient state-of-the-art interfaces. In my spare time, I enjoy designing and launching static web projects using semantic HTML5, modular CSS, and vanilla JavaScript for interactive features. I make sure to incorporate responsive design, use Git for version control, and optimize everything for different browsers. Coding is more than just a pastime for me—it’s a part of who I am!

Plus, I’m proud to carry on a family tradition that spans over two centuries of rich cultural heritage in Romania, all while reimagining it to tackle the challenges we face in the 21st century.

About This Website

Hosted on GitHub — the world’s leading platform for collaborative development and code transparency — this space is more than just a personal website: it’s a lively Progressive Web App (PWA) that acts as a colorful archive of technocultural insights. With features like offline access, RSS syndication, a stylish liquid glass UI, and eye-catching cinematic gradients, it offers users a delightful, multisensory experience that blends technology with creativity. Unlike other blogs, this space serves as a public repository: a channel for regular updates, cultural journalism, and code transparency.

Think of it as a vibrant hub where you can find news, dive into the world of my essays, and discover facets of the future. It’s all about engaging with ideas that go beyond just the usual back-and-forth, helping us reconnect with the true spirit of art nouveau in our digital world. Check out the V-Box section, where I’ve handpicked a great teaser from my YouTube channel Symbolic Echoes.

This site is more then a part of the network; it’s a heartfelt nod to our algorithm-driven world. Its creator isn’t just talking the talk—he’s walking the walk, having crafted every single line of code himself. From service workers to semantic markup, each detail showcases my genuine dedication to creating something meaningful online. Plus, users can easily subscribe via RSS news aggregators, install the site as a standalone app, and enjoy delving into its content across devices and modes—reinforcing its role as a decentralized, expressive archive of technocultural reflection.
