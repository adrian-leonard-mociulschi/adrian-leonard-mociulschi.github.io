About Me

I'm an academic and a writer from Romania, and I've had the pleasure of publishing a five-part series of essays with Curtea Veche Publishing. Think of it as a constellation of ideas that come together to create a symbolic pentagram of knowledge. My intellectual journey has been influenced by the cultural perspectives of thinkers like René Berger and Pierre Bourdieu, along with the visionary insights of Isaac Asimov and Arthur C. Clarke.

This principle fuels my mission: to forge exciting connections between culture and technology, literature and algorithms, memory and the future. It’s like a quiet revolution in the tech world—one that’s friendly to evolved algorithms and values merit above all. I’m passionate about promoting ethical AI and nurturing a posthuman ecology of meaning.

Plus, I’m proud to carry on a family tradition that spans over two centuries of rich cultural heritage in Romania, all while reimagining it to tackle the challenges we face in the 21st century.

About This Website

Hosted on GitHub, this platform is more than just your typical personal website—it’s a lively Progressive Web App (PWA) that acts as a colorful archive of technocultural insights. With features like offline access, RSS syndication, a stylish liquid glass UI, and eye-catching cinematic gradients, it offers users a delightful, multisensory experience that blends technology with creativity. Unlike other blogs, this space serves as a public repository: a channel for regular updates, cultural journalism, and code transparency.

Think of it as a vibrant hub where you can find news, dive into the world of my essays, and discover facets of the future. It’s all about engaging with ideas that go beyond just the usual back-and-forth, helping us reconnect with the true spirit of art nouveau in our digital world. Check out the V-Box section, where I’ve handpicked some educational video clips from my YouTube channel Symbolic Echoes.

“Scientia et ars, in unum resonant — sine hac, illa muta est.”
(Knowledge and art resonate as one — without the former, the latter is mute.)

This site is already part of the network; it’s a heartfelt nod to our algorithm-driven world. Its creator isn’t just talking the talk—he’s walking the walk, having crafted every single line of code himself. From service workers to semantic markup, each detail showcases my genuine dedication to creating something meaningful online. Plus, users can easily subscribe via RSS news aggregators, install the site as a standalone app, and enjoy delving into its content across devices and modes — reinforcing its role as a decentralized, expressive archive of technocultural reflection.
