A Name, A Chronicle, A Presence — Official Teaser of the Website

The AI Revolution: Between Hope and Fear. ISF Conference

Revista Cultura • Published on November 18, 2025

The article unfolds as a reflective journey through the promises and anxieties surrounding artificial intelligence, framed by the recent ISF Conference in Bucharest. Far from being a mere technical upgrade, AI emerges here as a cultural phenomenon—a silent architect of new paradigms in finance, governance, and everyday life. The text captures the tension between speed and responsibility, efficiency and ethics, showing how algorithms, once confined to the backstage of computation, now step into the symbolic arena of trust and meaning. Romania’s role in this dialogue is portrayed not as peripheral but as actively engaged in shaping global conversations on digital transformation and regulatory frameworks. The narrative insists that technology is never neutral: it carries with it a demand for critical literacy, for a pedagogy of meaning that can safeguard human agency in an age of predictive models. Ultimately, the article argues that the future of AI is not about machines replacing humans, but about humans redefining themselves through the lens of technology—an invitation to inhabit the convergence of innovation and responsibility with lucidity and imagination.
